# Capital Project Selection

Pick which capital projects to fund — maximize NPV & strategic fit, hold down spend & portfolio risk — under a hard budget, dependencies, and mutually-exclusive bets. Binary (each project in or out); 4 objectives; combinatorial constraints.

**Prompt:** "Load the capital project selection problem and find the efficient frontier of funding plans that trade off NPV, cost, risk, and strategic fit within the $140M budget. Walk me through a few representative plans."
